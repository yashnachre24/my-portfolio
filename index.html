About

Video editing taught me that storytelling is not just about cutting clips — it is about creating emotion, rhythm, and connection. Through pacing, visuals, and sound design, I transform simple footage into stories that hold attention and make audiences feel something.

Working with creators like Ranveer Allahbadia showed me how powerful great storytelling and high-quality production can be. Collaborating with top creators in India helped me understand professional workflows, audience psychology, and how impactful content is created at scale.

I participated in Mumbai’s first AI Hackathon organized by Ishan Sharma and Nandini Agrawal. Competing with talented creators pushed my creativity and technical skills. I finished in the Top 2 — just a step away from the winning spot. The experience taught me that growth, learning, and respect earned along the journey often matter more than the trophy itself. This is only the beginning.
