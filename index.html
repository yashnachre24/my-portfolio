The Story

About
Me

Video editing taught me that storytelling isn't about cutting clips — it's about creating emotion, rhythm, and connection. Through pacing, visuals, and sound design, I transform simple footage into stories that hold attention and make audiences feel something.

Working with creators like Ranveer Allahbadia showed me how powerful great storytelling and high-quality production can be. Collaborating with top creators in India helped me understand professional workflows, audience psychology, and how impactful content is made at scale.

I participated in Mumbai's first AI Hackathon organised by Ishan Sharma and Nandini Agrawal. Competing with talented creators pushed my creativity and technical skills. I finished in the Top 2 — just a step away from the winning spot.
