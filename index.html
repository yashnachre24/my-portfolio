The story

About me

Editing taught me that storytelling isn't about cutting clips — it's about creating emotion, rhythm and connection. Through pacing, visuals and sound design, I turn simple footage into stories that hold attention and make audiences feel something.

Working with creators like Ranveer Allahbadia showed me how powerful great storytelling and high-quality production can be. Collaborating with top creators in India taught me professional workflows, audience psychology, and how impactful content is made at scale.

I competed in Mumbai's first AI Hackathon, organised by Ishan Sharma and Nandini Agrawal — and finished in the Top 2, a single step from the win.
