The story

About me

I'm Yash Nachre, a Video Editor, Travel Storyteller and AI Filmmaker with 6+ years of experience blending storytelling, technology and creativity. My journey began in 2019 editing videos on a mobile phone — and has grown into 3,500+ videos across YouTube, podcasts, branded content, short-form and AI-generated films.

Today I work as an Executive Video Editor at BeerBiceps SkillHouse while building my travel brand, CAN YOOU TRAVEL, creating cinematic documentaries that showcase India's culture, people and hidden stories. Beyond editing, I mentor aspiring creators through Edit School and experiment constantly with AI filmmaking.

What makes me different? I combine professional editing, cinematic storytelling, travel filmmaking and AI-powered workflows into one creative process. My goal isn't just to edit videos — it's to craft stories that people remember.
